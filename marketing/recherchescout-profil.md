## 3. Pitch-Strategie

### Was Du beantwortest (für hohe Erfolgs-Rate)

✅ **Daten-basierte Anfragen** („Stats on X", „Survey results for Y")
✅ **Trend-Stories** („How is EAA compliance changing X?")
✅ **Solo-Founder-Stories** („Bootstrapping in compliance niche")
✅ **Anti-Overlay-Sachartikel** („Are accessibility overlays effective?")
✅ **BFSG-spezifisch** (was nur du in DACH-Tiefe kannst)

❌ **Was du NICHT beantwortest**
- Anfragen außerhalb deiner echten Expertise (Glaubwürdigkeits-Risiko)
- Anfragen die nur „Quote-Hunting" sind (kein Story-Plot)
- Anfragen für englische Mass-Media wenn deine Daten DE-spezifisch sind

### Pitch-Template (300-400 chars max — Journalisten sind beschäftigt!)

```
Hi [Journalist Name],

Re: [Their Query Topic]

I'm Matthias Seba, founder of BFSG-Check — an automated accessibility 
scanner for German websites. I have aggregated data from 10,000+ scans 
showing [konkrete Daten].

Quick answer to your question: [Direct, useful 2-sentence answer]

Happy to share the full dataset (anonymized) or do a video call for 
deeper context.

Best,
Matthias
matthias-seba.de | bfsg-fix.de
```

### Beispiel-Pitches (Templates für häufige Themen)

**Thema: „How is the EAA changing accessibility compliance in Europe?"**
```
The EAA forces ~95% of German online shops to comply — but our data 
from 10,000 scans shows 89% still fail at color contrast alone. The 
abmahnung wave (cease-and-desist letters) in Germany since August 2025 
has issued an estimated 5,000+ letters against shops. Most SMBs don't 
know they're affected. Happy to share aggregated dataset.
```

**Thema: „Are accessibility overlays like AccessiBe effective?"**
```
Our scans found that sites paying for AccessiBe/UserWay overlays STILL 
fail core WCAG criteria — overlays don't fix underlying HTML problems. 
The German Überwachungsstellen (oversight bodies) and disability orgs 
have officially criticized overlay tools since 2023. Honest scanners + 
manual fixes are the only sustainable path.
```

**Thema: „Solo founder bootstrapping in compliance niche"**
```
Built BFSG-Check solo in 6 weeks using Claude Code multi-agent sprints. 
Bootstrapped to live with €0 marketing budget initially, now scaling 
with €20/day Google Ads. Average B2B SaaS CAC in DACH for niche 
compliance: €150-400 (our data). Happy to share founder lessons learned.
```

- Der Journalist hat dich aktiv gefragt (HARO-Source-Request)
